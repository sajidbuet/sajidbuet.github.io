BUET

When I graduated from BUET, I noticed a difference between the BUET logo of my testimonial and the original certificate. I realized that there was no standard logo of BUET. I tried tracing the logo from my certificate and create a vector image. In Wikipedia, the logo of BUET was a very poor rendition, and I decided to upload it in Wikipedia as BUET_logo.svg on 12 November 2009. I did an additional fix of the logo in request of IICT, BUET, and did some minor house keeping, but the version is still kept in Wikipedia. As wikipedia grew in popularity, it was interesting to see that more and more people started using this logo as an official BUET logo. Currently it is used in BUET website, and most official diaries, calendars and departmental websites.

Occasionally, additional logos become necessary, and BUET has no media department to maintain a repository of the logos. As such, I am uploading them here in my personal website.

The hex color used in BUET logo in the wikipedia article is AC1F24.
